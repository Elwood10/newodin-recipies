Lasagna

This is a tasty recipe found in an ancient tomb of bat like creatures

    Here how to make it, without using sonar
  1. First buy all the ingredients and have 'em ready to roll
  2. Cook the noodles until Al Dente'
  3. Grind everything else up but the cheese, and combine and bring to a boil, order don't matter a lick
  4. Layer sauce, noodles and cheese over and over again, until you run out
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for half an hour, pull it out, let it sit for a bit, cut and eat
